📖 What the numbers mean

🌍 01

Carbon footprint

The total greenhouse gas impact of your daily activities — measured in kg CO₂ equivalent (CO₂e) per week.

🔑 02

The big levers

Home energy, transport, food, shopping, and waste typically explain 80 %+ of a person's personal emissions.

📈 03

Why track weekly?

Weekly logs are easier to sustain than perfect accounting and still reveal whether your direction is improving.

🌱 04

Personalised cuts

The most effective action is one tied to your largest source and simple enough to repeat every week.
